Help

Welcome to Deep Sea Explorer!

Objective:

  • Explore fish underwater and learn about their size, weight, diet, lifespan, etc.
  • Click onto the fish to reveal their information.
  • Check your understanding of fishes by playing mini-games.
  • Mini-games can be accessed in-game on the top right of the screen.

Mini-Games:

Guess!

  • This mini-game requires you to guess what fish it is, given a piece of information.
  • Click on the fish and check to see if you were correct.
  • Press 'Next' to proceed to the next question or 'Exit' to quit playing.

Catch!

  • This is a mini-game that requires you to catch the most amount of fish you can in 15 seconds.
  • Click 'Start' then try to catch the most amount of fish you can.
  • Check how many fish you have caught then press 'Ok' to leave.
